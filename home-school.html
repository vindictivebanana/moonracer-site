Home and School Services

Support customized directly to where real-world skill execution happens daily.

School Services

  • Executive functioning: Supporting organizational and planning skills.
  • Sensory Regulation: Helping clients learn about their specific nervous system and finding tools that work.
  • Fine and Gross Motor Development: Motor planning, coordination, and strengthening.
  • Social Skills: Fostering authentic and mutually beneficial interpersonal interactions and relationships.

Home Services

Moonracer provides in-home services for a limited number of local clients of all ages.


Additionally, we are hosting our "get ready for school" camps at the end of this summer for children aged 4-5. This program is designed to ease transition into the classroom environment by targeting foundational development skills and will be led by an OT and SLP.
