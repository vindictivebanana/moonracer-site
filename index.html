Moonracer Therapy

Welcome to the Moonracer community!
Moonracer therapy empowers individuals to understand their own unique nervous systems and brains in order to develop sustainable skills and strategies that work with their differences, not against them.

Moonracer offers neuroaffirming occupational therapy and executive functioning coaching both online and in-person. These services are significantly enhanced by group sessions and access to a community where individuals support one another as we navigate the world together.

Please use the site headers at the top of the page to learn more about how Moonracer can support you or your child.
